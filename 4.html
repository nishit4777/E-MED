Tamtero 20 mg Tablet
Uses of Tamtero Tablet
Breast cancer

Tamtero Tablet side effects
  • Swelling
  • Loss of libido
  • Hot flashes
  • Vaginal discharge

    • How to use Tamtero Tablet
    Take this medicine in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor. Swallow it as a whole. Do not chew, crush or break it. Tamtero 20 mg Tablet may be taken with or without food, but it is better to take it at a fixed time.

    How Tamtero Tablet works
    Tamtero 20 mg Tablet is a hormone-based medication. It works by binding to estrogen receptors and blocking the effects of estrogen (a natural female hormone) in the breast tissue. This slows down the growth and multiplication of breast cancer cells.

    Tamtero Tablet related warnings
  • Alcohol
    Interaction with alcohol is unknown. Please consult your doctor.
  • Pregnancy
    Tamtero 20 mg Tablet is highly unsafe to use during pregnancy.Human and animal studies have shown significant adverse effects on the fetus. Please consult your doctor.
  • Lactation
    Tamtero 20 mg Tablet is probably unsafe to use during lactation. Limited human data suggest that the drug could represent a significant risk to the baby.
  • Driving
    Do not drive unless you are feeling well.
    Tamtero 20 mg Tablet may cause tiredness. This may affect your driving ability.
  • Kidney
    Tamtero 20 mg Tablet is probably safe to use in patients with kidney disease. Limited data available suggests that dose adjustment of Tamtero 20 mg Tablet may not be needed in these patients. Please consult your doctor.
  • Liver
    There is limited information available on the use of Tamtero 20 mg Tablet in patients with liver disease. Please consult your doctor.
